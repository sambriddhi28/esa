Hotel Policies

Fixed Rate Pricing

$25 per night + taxes for the first 6 nights. $11.25 per night after 6 nights.

Check-Out Time

Standard check-out time is 11:00 AM. Late checkout is available upon request. Contact the front desk for arrangements.

🚭

No Smoking Policy

All rooms are strictly non-smoking. Smoking in your room will result in a $250 cleaning penalty. Complete deposit forfeiture and immediate stay termination without refund.

Garbage Disposal

A trash room is located on each floor near the elevator. Please dispose of all garbage in the designated trash room. Do not leave trash outside your room or in hallways.

Linen Exchange

Fresh linens are available daily. You should visit the front desk for linen exchange.

Explore the Neighborhood

Great American Ball Park

Home of the Cincinnati Reds. Experience exciting baseball games and entertainment events.

Cincinnati Riverfront

Beautiful parks, walking trails, and scenic views along the Ohio River. Perfect for outdoor activities.

Roebling Suspension Bridge

Historic engineering marvel connecting Cincinnati to Kentucky. Great photo spot and pedestrian-friendly walkway.

Newport Aquarium

Explore thousands of aquatic animals and interactive exhibits. Located just across the river in Newport, Kentucky.

Downtown Cincinnati

Vibrant urban center with shopping, dining, galleries, and entertainment. Walk the historic streets.

Findlay Market & Historic District

Historic public market with fresh produce, local vendors, and restaurants. Experience authentic Cincinnati culture.

Housekeeping Service

🧹

Extended Stay Housekeeping

After your first 14 days of stay, complimentary housekeeping service is available upon request.

To request housekeeping service, simply visit the front desk or call. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Room Cleaning

Full room cleaning including bedroom, living area, bathroom, and kitchen. Fresh linens provided.

How to Request

Visit the front desk in person, call the front desk phone, or send an email with your preferred time.

Availability

Housekeeping is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Weekend service available by request.

Questions? Contact our 24/7 front desk for assistance.
